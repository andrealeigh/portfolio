TriNetX, Inc: Clinical Trials Feasibility Product

TriNetX is a B2B healthcare startup in Cambridge, MA. Their product is a web-based application for clinical trial feasibility. Their user base includes Pharma/CROs, HCOs, and in-house Clinical Operations.

I redesigned the UX/UI of major features within their application. While I am not able to publicly share my design work, I would be happy to present on the following in a controlled interview setting:

  • Collaboration with Product, Development, and Clinical Operations
  • Low fidelity mockups: Paper/pencil sketches; Balsamiq wireframes
  • High fidelity mockups: Sketch and Adobe Illustrator mockups
  • Internal and external user research
  • Clickable prototypes: Invision
  • User testing of beta product
  • UI Review with Development and Product

Testive, Inc: School Analytics Product

Testive is a B2C education startup in Boston, MA. Their products include a variety of web-based applications that help students and their mentors prepare for the SAT/ACT.

As the primary UX/UI designer at Testive, I led the design process for a product called School Analytics, including from initial ideation to deployment. The product’s user base included school teachers and guidance counselors who wanted a way to track the progress of their students who used the Testive app for SAT/ACT prep:

  • Ideation meetings with Head of Product, Head of Engineering, and Director of School Partnerships
  • Low fidelity mockups: Paper/pencil wireframes, whiteboard sketches
  • High fidelity mockups: Adobe Illustrator mockups
  • User Research & Testing from teachers
  • HTML/SCSS development in collaboration with development team
