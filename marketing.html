Role: I redesigned major features of their application within the context of a front-end overhaul project. While I am not able to publicly share my design work, I would be happy to present on the following in a controlled interview setting:

  • Clickable prototypes: Invision

Testive, Inc: School Analytics Product

Overview: Testive is a B2C education startup in Boston, MA. Their products include multiple web-based applications that help students and mentors prepare for the SAT/ACT.

Role: As the primary UX/UI designer at Testive, I led the design process for a product called School Analytics. The product’s user base included school teachers and guidance counselors who wanted a way to track their students’ progress who used the Testive app for SAT/ACT prep:

  • Ideation meetings with Head of Product, Head of Engineering, and Director of School Partnerships
  • Low fidelity mockups: Paper/pencil wireframes, whiteboard sketches
  • High fidelity mockups: Adobe Illustrator mockups
  • HTML/SCSS development & collaboration with development team
