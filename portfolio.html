TriNetX, Inc: Clinical Trials Feasibility Product

Overview: TriNetX is a B2B healthcare startup in Cambridge, MA. Their product is a web-based application for clinical trial feasibility.

Role: I redesigned major features of their application within the context of a complete front-end overhaul. While I am not able to publicly share my design work, I would be happy to present on the following in a controlled interview setting:

  • Collaboration with Product, Development, and Clinical Operations
  • Low fidelity mockups: Paper/pencil sketches; Balsamiq wireframes
  • High fidelity mockups: Sketch and Adobe Illustrator mockups
  • User research & testing of beta product
  • Clickable prototypes: Invision

Testive, Inc: School Analytics Product

Overview: Testive is a B2C education startup in Boston, MA. Their products include several web-based applications that help students and their mentors prepare for the SAT/ACT.

Role: As the primary UX/UI designer at Testive, I led the design process for a product called School Analytics. The product’s user base included school teachers and guidance counselors who wanted a way to track their students’ progress on the Testive app:

  • Ideation meetings with Head of Product, Head of Engineering, and Director of School Partnerships
  • User research with potential active users
  • Low fidelity mockups: Paper/pencil wireframes, whiteboard sketches
  • High fidelity mockups: Adobe Illustrator mockups
  • HTML/SCSS development & collaboration with development team

Open Source Project: Call Congress Now

Role: I led the design process from start to end and also developed the HTML/SCSS. I have included key points in the design process:

  • Defining the idea: User personas and storyboarding
  • User research
  • Design phase
  • Development & testing
  • Analysis & next steps
