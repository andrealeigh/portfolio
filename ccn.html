Call Congress Now: From idea to web application

Overview

Call Congress Now is a responsive web app I designed and co-developed. The purpose of the application is to encourage citizens to call their Representatives and Senators by providing calling information in a user-friendly way.

The Challenge

In the weeks following America’s 2016 election night, I made a vow to get more politically involved. I read through tweets about the impact of picking up the phone and dialing Senators and Representatives. My friend and I were intrigued. We started looking into how to contact Congress, and we were startled by how little information about it existed. Then we realized that we could do something about it.

The Solution

We decided to make a responsive web app that would be create a user-friendly way to call members of Congress? How do we motivate our peers to pick up the phone?
